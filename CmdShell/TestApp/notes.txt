=== Argument
{path}					required
{path?}					optional
{path=C:\projects}		with default parameter

=== Options
{--report}				boolean options
{--report|r}
{--report|R}
{--r|report}
{--R|report}
{--report|R=}								required options
{--report|R=report.txt}						with default value
{--report|R=report.txt:Make the report}

clean {path} {--recursive|r} {--mask|m=*.txt}
